About Me

As a former e-Commerce manager, I have a unique understanding of the industry and a proven track record of success in driving business growth and delivering results. However, I found a love for the technical side of e-Commerce and decided to switch career course and become a developer. I'm now skilled in HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript, and have a deep understanding of the SASS platform Shopify. My problem-solving abilities and strong communication skills make me a valuable asset to any development team.


I received my BSc degree from Loughborough University where I played cricket for the MCCU academy. My experience has taught me the importance of adaptability and people management. I am a team player who is able to work effectively with cross-functional teams and to drive projects to completion.


I'm excited for the challenges and opportunities ahead as I continue my career as a developer. I look forward to making a meaningful impact in any new role.
